Buffalo Chicken Mac & Cheese

Classic comfort food dish with a twist.

Packed with protein, this dish takes two of my favorite things and makes them into a healthy-ish version to enjoy. Made without that big brick of processed cheese, you would never know this is made with cottage cheese and protein pasta.

Ingredients

Marinade:

  • 1.25lb Chicken Breast or Thights, cut into bite sized pieces.
  • 3 T Red Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 1 T Soy Sauce

Pasta:

  • 1 box of Protein Pasta

Sauce:

  • 1 24oz tub of Fat Free or 2% Cottage Cheese
  • 1 Brick of 1/3-Fat Cream Cheese
  • 2 T Soy Sauce
  • 2 T Honey
  • 1/3 C Red Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 2 T Cheddar Cheese Powder*
  • 1.5lbs of chicken breast or thighs

Steps

  1. Cut raw chicken into bite sized pieces. Cover with 1/3C buffalo sauce, 1 Tablespoon of honey, and 2 Tablespoons of soysauce. Let sit for about 20 mins.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare box of pasta according to directions. Strain and save about 1/4-cup of pasta water.
  3. Heat oil of your choice on med-high heat. Add chicken and cook through. Take out chicken.
  4. Reducing heat to low, add cottage cheese, cream cheese, cheese powder, and melt-- stirring constantly over low heat.
  5. Once cheeses are melted, add pasta, chicken and stir. Add pasta water 1 T at a tine until sauce adheres to pasta.
  6. Enjoy!
