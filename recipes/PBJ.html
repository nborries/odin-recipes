Peanut Butter & Jelly

Learn how to make this American Classic

This sandwich features two staples of american culture: peanut butter and jelly. Featured on white bread and paired with a glass of milk, this sandwich reminds me so much of my childhood.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of White Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Flavor of choice)

    Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one or both slices of bread.
  2. On one piece of bread, add jelly and spread.
  3. Join both slices of bread together, with peanut butter and jelly sides touching.
  4. Enjoy!
