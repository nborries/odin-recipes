Wild Rice Soup

Warm, earthy soup perfect for any cool day.

Crack out the dutch oven or large pot for this one. Make a quick (but tasty) soup to serve on those crisp, autumn and winter days. With the wild rice, ham, and rosemary, this earthy soup makes for a great side or main dish.

    Ingredients

  • 3 C Wild Rice (I use Uncle Ben's)
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 1-2 Celery Stalks, diced
  • 1-2 Medium Carrots, diced
  • 1/2 c butter (one stick)
  • 1/2 c flour
  • 3 C Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 C of Cream/milk
  • 1/4 C Chopped slivered almonds

    Steps

  1. Cook rice according to packaged directions.
  2. Melt butter over medium heat. Add onion, celery and carrot. Stir until fragrant and tender.
  3. Mix in flour. Stir well for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add broth and rice, stirring until slighty thickened.
  5. Add cream, salt, rosemary, ham and almonds. Stir. Bring to boil
  6. Reduce heat, let simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Enjoy!
